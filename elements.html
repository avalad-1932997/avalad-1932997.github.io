Twenty One Pilots accepted Best Pop Group Performance Grammy in their underwear

Standing in their boxers as if it was the most normal thing in the world, Joseph then turned back time to the good old days and began regaling a story from their youth in Colombus Ohio, when a group of their friends were all chilling out at home and watching the Grammys. 'We noticed that every single one of us was in our underwear,' he recalled.

'And seriously, Josh turned to me - and we were no-one at that time - and he said to me: "If we ever go to the Grammys, if we ever win a Grammy, we should receive it just like this.' 'So not only is this amazing, but I want everyone at home whose watching to know that you can be next,' Joseph concluded. 'So watch out, OK, because anyone from anywhere can do anything. And this is that.'
